Oxfam Fair Trade

Oxfam-Worldshops works on just rules for international trade. We believe fair trade gives producers in the South the chance of gaining independence, wich will contribute to more economic development.

Millions of producers in the South do not get a fair chance on the world market. Because of this they often live in poverty.

Trade, when it happens fairly, is a powerful lever for sustainable development. That's why Oxfam World Stores wants just rules for international trade.

By working together farmers share the risks, improve their negotiating position and are a more powerful force on the international trading field. In this we support them.
