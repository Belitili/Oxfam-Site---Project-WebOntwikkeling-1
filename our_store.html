Our Store

Our small store here in Heverlee is located centrally, near the train station. Inside you will find a wide range of Oxfam Fair Trade products and the friendly face of one of our volunteers. If there is something that you can't find in our store, don't hesitate to ask them for help, in our store, or via the contact form. Hopefully until soon!

Oxfam World Stores aren't just speciality stores for quality products of fair trade. Each store is also an information centre and a meeting place for everyone who wants to work together to form a more just world. Perhaps you too feel the urge to start your own Oxfam campaign or event. If so feel free to take a look on our 'for groups' page or contact us directly. Would you prefer to become one of our volunteers directly, then just keep reading.

Our Volunteers

The 8000 world store volunteers keep the stores going, but they do so much more. They spread our story of fair trade, make connections with other organisation, introduce young people to fair trade, and so on. Do you feel the need to make a difference, then continue reading about volunteers here (dutch) or contact us directly.
